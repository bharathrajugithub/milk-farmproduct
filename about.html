About Us

Farm Connect is an Indian Organic Certified Dairy Farm.

We at our farms empower farmers to produce organic dairy products in an environmentally sustainable and socially responsible way. The health and well-being of our cows is a key priority and is paramount to our holistic organic farming ethos. Not only to animal welfare, our methodologies contributes significantly to soil regeneration, water conservation, farmer upliftment and an impactful change in the whole ecosystem.

Dr. G. N. S. Reddy

Co-Founder, Chairman

Dr. GNS Reddy, the pioneer of organic milk in India, currently the Founder and Director of Kalpasiri Farms and Foods Private Limited (Farm Connect) with a mission to produce best in class milk from rural farms of India.


As a child, he always felt more drawn to animal healthcare than human healthcare. Further, his passion and love for animals led him to study veterinary sciences at Vijaya College, Bangalore.

During graduation, Dr. Reddy only had two cows for the students to treat, work on and learn from, which meant there were 30 to 40 students lining up around one cow. This was never enough so during the five years of his veterinary sciences education he never took a vacation. Dr. Reddy stayed at school during all the holidays to be able to have the time to handle the cows and other animals. Doing this allowed him to gain more practical, hands-on training and he graduated with more experience than many practicing veterinary doctors. When Dr. Reddy returned to his family and village, the stark gap in services for farmers started made him uncomfortable. But he did not know what he could do about it.

A lecture by Dr. Manibhai Desai, a Gandhian, who took over the Ashram after Gandhi, influenced him. Dr. Desai identified the same problem of a lack of services for farmers and found a way to address it through his organization, Bharatiya Agro Industries Foundation (BAIF). Dr. Reddy joined Dr. Desai and worked at BAIF for many years. Murthy Muniyappa

Co-founder, Managing Director and a Farmer

Mr Murthy completed his B.E/B.Tech in Computer Science from SJCE College, University of Mysore in the year 1993. This was followed by 28 years of experience in a variety of roles in the world of technology, working at various companies like Sasken, Mobileum, Above solutions and currently Life 9 Systems Private Limited.

He specializes in leadership and mentoring in areas such as user experience, product development, business development, technology strategy and strategic account management.

Mr Murthy went on to start Life9 Systems Pvt., Ltd., an AIoT product Engineering and solution provider with the vision of facilitating digital transformation, where he holds the position of CEO. He also holds influential positions in many IT solutions and consulting firms. He is leveraging the 28 years of experience and skills to cater to fields such as Agriculture, Logistics, transportation, education and smart cities. Life9 Systems has established itself as a niche player in the digital transformation movement.

Hailing from a family with an extensive background in farming and being an avid traveller has led Mr. Murthy to adopt the stance of “Food as a medicine”. He is a strong supporter of natural and organic farming practices in order to provide society with healthy non-GMO produce. Simultaneously, he believes conserving natural soil, in being gentle on ‘Mother Earth’ by adopting farming practices that do not leave the land barren or toxic with chemicals.

He dreams of uplifting and empowering the rural farmers so that they may be recognized as Entrepreneurs. As the Managing Director of Farm Connect (Kalpasiri Farms), he is now on a journey to equip farmers with digital technologies that makes Dairy farming a much more reliable source of income while also making the processes behind dairy farming less intensive, more efficient and less hands-on. This in turn would help shape the future of Agriculture business in India.

However, it does not stop at helping the farmers and enriching their lives. Like us humans, animals are sentient beings and their mental state has an effect on their physical condition. This means that a stressed cow would lead to lower milk production, impaired immunity and other issues. In order to get the highest quality of milk, besides natural fodder and cattle feed, Mr. Murthy is working towards bettering their environment with facilities like good housing and enough land space and enrichment items to provide them with an ideal life. This is in addition to the hygiene and health treatment provided by experienced farmers and veterinarians to avoid heat stress and other medical issues.

With the current technologies and data on hand, Mr. Murthy and his team are in the R&D phase of trying to come up with measurable parameters that help them determine the happiness level of a cow so that they can better understand and help the cows.
