Integrated organic dairy project

High level of hygiene, drudgery free setup with minimal human interventions. Happy, stress free, cohesive and organic environment for cows. Extensive training, guidance & supervision support. Zero involvement of Chemicals, Pesticides, Growth Hormones, Antibiotics, non organic substances, adulterants etc. in general everything is ‘Natural’

1. Rearing

  • Respecting need for hygienic surrounding and freedom of movement and promoting natural life cycle.
  • Provide best dairy process, technology and Cow Health Care system at farms.
  • 22 varieties of fodder are organically grown in our farms.
  • Everything is ‘Natural’

2. Milking

  • Automated milking Technology with minimal human touch ensuring for cow’s comfort and safety.
  • Scientifically cooled in Bulk Milk Coolers immediately, to 400 C
  • Raw milk maintained hygienically in cold chain to preserve all essential Nutrients and enzymes.
  • Milking machines and Coolers thoroughly washed after every single use.

3. Processing

  • Milk is tested for different parameters immediately in the processing center.
  • Raw Milk is further just Packed and sent for delivery.
  • All other products such as Ghee, Butter, Paneer are produced in traditional methods to preserve the nutritional value.
  • Zero involvement of chemicals, pesticides, growth hormones, antibiotics, nonorganic substances, adulterants.

4. Packing

  • Raw Milk is Packed immediately after testing for 11 different parameters.
  • All Mechanized packing to ensure no human touch

5. Delivery

  • FRESH MILK delivered to door step quickly, maintaining cold chain throughout.

6. Technology

  • Backed by Life9 Systems Pvt., Ltd., Farm Connect continuously monitors every stage from fodder management, cow health, milk cooling, processing, logistics to delivery with the implementation of IoT devices and Sensors ensuring Best of the quality product reaches every customer and making itself unique as a Technology driven Diary Industry
